The Artistry Behind Salon Looha

Meet the visionaries who curate our signature aesthetics and elevate the standard of luxury beauty.

Dimalka Nuwanthi

Founder & Master Stylist

Embarking on my journey in the salon industry in 2015, I have dedicated over a decade to mastering the art of beauty and transformations. My passion and expertise in creating breathtaking bridal transformations earned me the prestigious National Award for Bridal Makeup in 2018. Driven by a commitment to continuous growth and innovation, I expanded my artistry into advanced nail care, which led to winning the renowned Cuccio Nail Special Award in 2025. Today, I combine years of experience with certified expertise to offer paralleled beauty services that make every client feel extraordinary.

Philosophy

"Every individual possesses a unique, inherent beauty. My philosophy is to enhance that natural elegance using the highest quality products and advanced techniques, creating a timeless look that builds ultimate confidence and celebrates who you truly are."

Certifications

  • National Certificate in Beautician (NVQ Level 4)
  • Diploma in Salon Management
  • National Certificate in Hair Stylist / Hairdressing (NVQ Level 4)
